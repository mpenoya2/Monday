My Week

My week allows users to see what due dates and deadlines are coming up in the current week. They can also travel to future weeks, or previous weeks if necessary to look over past projects. This is useful to keep track of all projects that are due, and make sure that they are completed on time, or review past work.

Owners of the Boards will be able to edit the deadlines if needed. Other users can hide boards if they don't want to see it at the given time, but can easily reverse the decision if necessary.
