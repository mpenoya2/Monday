      Integrations can be made within the Boards to page to use other company collaborative products, such as Google Drive, Gmail, Slack, and more. This allows users to connect all potential files and messages in one place, and can be done very simply from the Monday Boards page.

      Integrations may need to be turned off, if your company no longer uses said integration, or it is no longer necessary for the project at hand. It can be quickly turned off from the Boards Page.
